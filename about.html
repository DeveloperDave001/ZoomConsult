OUR STORY

FROM VISION TO REALITY.

Founded in 2004, Zoom Consults began with a simple belief: great spaces don't happen by accident. They're the result of meticulous planning, skilled execution, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

What started as a small interior design practice has evolved into a full-service design and build firm with three specialized divisions. Today, we're proud to have delivered over 250 projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

Our approach is rooted in collaboration. We listen first, design second, and build with precision. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries while staying true to our core values: integrity, craftsmanship, and client satisfaction.
